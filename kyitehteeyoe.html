Yangon Kyaikhtiyo Bago Mawlamyine

Tour Package (Yangon-Kyaikhtiyo-Bago-Mawlamyine)

Myanmar has many things to do and to see, so if your time is limited, this 6-day itinerary will cover the main highlights of Yangon, Hpa-An and Mawlamyaing which are Lower Myanmar city. By using a private coach, you’ll get to spend more flexible time in each region.

  • Half day Yangon city highlights tour
  • Golden Rock
  • Exploring colorful Hpa-An
  • Private coach transportation between Yangon, Hpa-An and Mawlamyaing

Your Journey Day By Day


Day 1 : Arrival Yangon


Arrive at Yangon airport.Drive through the Colonial Style Buildings downtown, the Chinatown area, the Indian quarter, Sule Pagoda and City Hall. Drive around the Kandawgyi Park, Chauk Htat Gyi Pagoda and enjoy sunset at Shwedagon Pagoda.


Day 2 Yangon – Bago – Kyaikhtiyo


Enjoy breakfast at hotel. Early morning, drive out of Yangon to the ancient capital of Bago. Visit Shwemawdaw Pagoda, the highest pagoda in Myanmar and Bago’s golden landmark.


Day 3 Kyaiktiyo – Hpa-An – Mawlamyaing


After breakfast, walk down the mountain and return by truck to your car/minibus, or alternatively walk all the way down to the base camp at Kinpun (leaving very early morning if you choose to do this).


Day 4 Mawlamyaing


Breakfast at hotel. Depart Mawlamyaing and drive south to Win Sein Tawya, where the largest reclining Buddha in the world is under construction. Stop will be at Thanbyuzayat, Thanbyuzayat War Cemetery.


Day 5 Mawlamyaing – Yangon


Early breakfast at hotel. Return to Yangon by road. On the way, stop in Thaton and you will visit Shwezayan Pagoda, home to a famous standing Buddha stele in the classic Mon style.


Day 6 Yangon – Departure


Enjoy breakfast at hotel. This morning, transfer to Yangon Airport for your departure flight…
